This was a beautiful moment captured by one of our fellow "fans in arms" at this year's Hoobastank tour in Japan. Doug Robb has such a powerful way of capturing an audience. I can feel his presense from here! I can't wait to see the band in May!

West Coast and East Coast meet, but over seas! Hoobastank and Sevendust collaborated on a performance at the US Yongsan Military base in Seoul Korea. According to our sources, it was an epic dose of nystalgia made new again by this mix of two indie rock bands from the 90's and early 2000's.

Hoobastank makes a special appearance at Fort Carson and swings by to perform at the state fair later. I love to see them making the most of their travels and making the most out of venues large and small. They love doing what they do, and we love listening to them.

An epic MTV event hosted in Bangkok, Thailand. Hoobastank got to perform, and be celebrated with, other artists such as Kelly Roland, Korn and Wang Leehom. The theme of the night was "futuristic Asia," but based on the band's atire, it's safe to say they wanted to be planted firmly in the present.

Ah, Bayfest in the early 2010's, those were the years! If you were around in the music scene at this time, you got to see a lot of the alt rock bands in their glory, just before the music genre started it's decline from mainstreem music. Hoobastank and 3 Doors Down performed along side the likes of Blake Shelton, Toby Keith, Ludacris, and Wiz Khalifa at BayFest 2011. Man, it's crazy how times have changed.
